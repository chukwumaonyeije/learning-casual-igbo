## Intended learning

Build one usable exchange after a greeting: say your name and ask another person’s name. This should work as a small bridge into family, church, or community conversation.

## Phrases introduced

- `Aha m bụ [name].` — My name is [name].
- `Aha gị bụ gịnị?` — What is your name?

## After the lesson, I should be able to

- Greet someone, then introduce myself using my own name.
- Ask their name using the complete phrase.
- Notice the dotted vowels in `bụ`, `gị`, and `gịnị`.
