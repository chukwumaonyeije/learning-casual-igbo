DIALOGUE 0002 · FRIEND

A short friendly check-in

IgboMeaningReading help
You: Kedu?How are you? / How is it?Short, warm check-in.
Friend: Adị m mma.I am fine.Keep the dotted ị.
You: Ọ dị mma.It is good / That is good.Notice dotted ọ and ị.

Cultural note

Kedu? is a useful casual starter, but friendly greetings vary across households and dialects. Listen for the shape your people use.

Short variation

Ụtụtụ ọma! — Good morning!

Practice prompt

Say both roles. Then answer Kedu? without looking at the page.

Naturalness improves by using a small set often, not by memorizing a long list.
