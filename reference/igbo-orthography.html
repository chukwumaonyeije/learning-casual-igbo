Orthography at a glance

Standard written Igbo has 36 alphabet units. Some are single letters; some are digraphs—two written characters read as one unit.

The eight vowels

aeiịoọuụ

Dotted vowels: ị, ọ, ụ. The dot is part of the spelling, not decoration.

Alphabet units

a b ch d e f g gb gh gw h i ị j k kp kw l m n ṅ nw ny o ọ p r s sh t u ụ v w y z

Reading habit

Scan first for a digraph, then for dotted vowels. Do not import English spelling rules: in Igbo, spelling is a much more direct guide to how a word is read.
