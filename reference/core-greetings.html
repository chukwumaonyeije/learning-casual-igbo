CASUAL IGBO PHRASEBOOK · CARD 01

Core greetings

Start warm, simple, and observant. A respectful greeting plus a title travels well.

IgboMeaningUse
NdewoHello / a respectful greetingGood default for one person, especially an elder.
Ndewo nuHello, everyoneGreeting a group.
Ụtụtụ ọmaGood morningMorning greeting.
Ehihie ọmaGood afternoonDaytime greeting.
Mgbede ọmaGood eveningEvening greeting.
Kedu?How are you? / How is it?Casual, friendly check-in; usage can vary by family and region.
Adị m mmaI am fineA useful response to a wellbeing question.
Respect move: add a known title—Mama, Papa, or a family-appropriate title—after Ndewo.
Pronunciation is best reinforced by a fluent speaker; keep the written units intact.
